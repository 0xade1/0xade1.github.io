Reading the exploit author blog https://cfreal.github.io/carpe-diem-cve-2019-0211-apache-local-root.html, it says this exploit needs to obtain R/W privileges on a worker process, and it uses a PHP UAF 0-day exploit.

Since mod_prefork is often used in combination with mod_php, it seems natural to exploit the vulnerability through PHP. CVE-2019-6977 (exploit) would be a perfect candidate, but it was not out when I started writing the exploit. I went with a 0day UAF in PHP 7.x (which seems to work in PHP5.x as well):

Let’s verify if the PHP version is vulnerable to CVE-2019-6977 (NIST). According to the CVE description, vulnerable version are PHP before 5.6.40, 7.x before 7.1.26, 7.2.x before 7.2.14, and 7.3.x before 7.3.1.
